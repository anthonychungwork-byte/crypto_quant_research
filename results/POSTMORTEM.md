# Postmortem

This document records strategies that failed any gate of the 6-stage SOP.

Each failed strategy gets an entry containing:

- **Stage** at which the failure was detected
- **Metric(s)** that triggered the kill criterion
- **Hypothesis revision attempted** (if any) before retirement
- **Lesson** captured for the next research cycle

A failed strategy is a successful execution of the methodology: the SOP exists precisely to catch the strategies that would not survive live trading. The postmortem is therefore part of the deliverable, not an admission of weakness.
